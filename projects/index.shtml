Machine learning to select optimal question sequences
Python Machine learning

My final year project is titled:

Using Multi-Armed Bandits to Select Optimal Question Sequences for Students in an Online Education Platform

The project aims to train machine learning models using a multi-armed bandit strategy to complete task 4 of the NeurIPS 2020 Education Challenge

The task is to give new students a sequence of 10 questions, observing their responses after every question, and then predict their performance (pass/fail) on the remainder of questions they attempted.

Oscar: NLP to schedule doctor's appointments

Oscar was a group project as part of my second year of university.

The project is a proof-of-concept that we could send reminders to patients and then use Apache's OpenNLP to process patient queries like rescheduling an appointment and then generate responses to them.
